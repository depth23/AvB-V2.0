About Us

While in high school, I began working at Sundance directly beneath the Executive Chef. I then went on to work at the Little America Hotel under the once Chef of the Year, Executive Pastry Chef, Hans Zulliger. It was then that I realized my love of baking. Seeing Hans work was mind blowing. Everything he made was a work of art and I aspired to do the same. I began making delicious creations using old school methods with only the freshest ingredients and all from scratch. I'd love for you to stop by for a tasting. Or, if you'd like to place an order, call (801) 420-2011. I bake delicious creations to show my love of baking so contact me to taste the delectability of my passion.
